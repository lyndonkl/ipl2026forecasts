### Dew Impact Analysis

Dew becomes a major factor at Eden Gardens from overs 12–13 onwards in the second innings. With temperatures expected to cool from 30°C to 25–26°C and humidity in the 65–75% range, the ball will become slippery. This affects:

- **Spinners:** Grip diminishes significantly. Leg-spinners and off-spinners lose control on length and line. Death-over bowling by spinners becomes high-risk.
- **Pacers:** Reverse swing becomes difficult. Conventional seam bowling more reliable than attempts at reverse swing.
- **Fielders:** Catching becomes hazardous with a wet ball. Misfields and dropped catches likely.
- **Batters (second innings):** Ball skids onto the bat more readily, aiding strokeplay and reducing bowling effectiveness.
