### Scenario Edge Summary

**KKR Structural Strengths:**
- Balanced top order (Allen + Rahane + Green + Raghuvanshi)
- Spin pairing (Varun + Narine) on home ground at Eden Gardens

**SRH Structural Strengths:**
- Elite opening pair (Abhishek Sharma + Travis Head) — both in exceptional form
- World-class finisher (Klaasen) with 180+ SR career
- Strong death bowler (Harshal Patel) in Cummins' absence

**Match Conditional Factors:**
1. **Varun Chakravarthy's confidence:** Game 2 conceded 0/48; Game 6 is redemption. If Varun executes mystery variations, KKR middle overs are strong. If Varun defensive, SRH LHB openers dominate.

4. **Toss winner's choice:** Likely bowl first (securing dry batting conditions). First-innings team benefits but faces dew in defense.
