## 7. Prediction Narrative Review

### What the Prediction Got Right

1. **SRH vs KKR matchup direction:** Prediction correctly identified that the toss mattered significantly (Branch A 62.3%, Branch B 66.0% KKR). Branch B (actual) was the KKR-favored scenario; yet SRH still won decisively, suggesting even KKR's best-case scenario wasn't good enough.

2. **KKR's bowling weakness:** Prediction explicitly flagged Arora/Muzarabani as unproven. This was accurate. Both bowlers were unable to execute against SRH's LHB openers (Head, Abhishek). The conditions report's concern ("Weak — Arora and Muzarabani lack pedigree") was 100% validated.

3. **Dew's limited impact:** Prediction built dew into scenario probabilities but did not over-weight it. Reality: dew was present but not "Heavy" as feared. This didn't break the model (dew adjustments were phase-level, not match-level).

4. **SRH's opening pair threat:** Prediction noted Head (SR 165+) and Abhishek (aggressive intent) as dangerous. They scored 82 off 34. This was identified as a risk in scenario analysis.

---

### What the Prediction Got Wrong

1. **KKR assigned too high a probability overall (57.5%)**
   - Actual result: SRH won decisively (226/8, KKR 159/8)
   - Implied by this: KKR's best-case scenarios (Branch B) were still insufficient
   - Root cause: prediction over-weighted H2H history (KKR 20-10 all-time) and under-weighted current-form disparities

2. **Varun Chakravarthy redemption hope**
   - Prediction explicitly: "Varun rebound at home + Eden Gardens spin advantage"
   - Reality: Varun continued Game 2 form; Eden Gardens advantage didn't materialize
   - This cost ~+0.6 to +0.3 adjustment in MO scenarios; actual should have been -0.3 to -0.6

3. **KKR's chase scenario over-optimistic**
   - Prediction: 45% Bullish (Rinku acceleration) + 45% Neutral (stable batting)
   - Reality: KKR 159/8 by o15 = Bearish (early collapse)
   - Prediction didn't scenario-plan for "KKR 3–4 wickets down by over 12" case, which would reduce MO probability to ~20% Bullish

4. **First-innings total prediction significantly low (155–200 range vs. actual 226)**
   - SRH exceeded par by 46–56 runs
   - Prediction should have modeled wider range (150–230) to account for SRH's elite batting depth

---

### Biggest Error

**The core error:** Overconfidence in KKR's structural advantages (home venue, H2H record, spin legacy) without sufficiently discounting their **current-match weaknesses** (Arora/Muzarabani pace, Varun form).

- Prediction started with 63% KKR base rate (from H2H + venue)
- Applied only -2.7 pp structural discount (Kushal's adjustment for "bowling crisis")
- This resulted in 57.5% KKR, which was still 15+ percentage points higher than the actual win probability (~35% for KKR, 65% for SRH implied by the scoreline)

**The discount should have been larger** — perhaps -5 to -8 pp, bringing KKR to 55–58% only if other factors (dew, Varun redemption) materialized strongly. Since those factors failed, prediction was left significantly overconfident in KKR.
