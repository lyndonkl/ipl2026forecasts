## Pitch Profile

- **Pitch type:** FLAT batting surface with good pace and carry
- **Surface composition:** Red soil — provides consistent bounce, ball comes
  nicely onto the bat
- **Expected behavior by phase:**
  - Early overs (1–6): True pace and bounce. New ball may swing briefly
    under lights, but movement typically lasts only 2–3 overs. Suits
    aggressive batting from ball one.
  - Middle overs (7–15): Pitch flattens further. Spin gets minimal
    assistance — Wankhede is not a spin-friendly venue. Good batters
    score freely through this phase.
  - Death overs (16–20): Remains true. Batters can hit through the line.
    Short square boundaries at Wankhede make death-over bowling extremely
    challenging.
- **Historical average first innings score:** 170–175
- **Assessment:** Classic Wankhede — batting-friendly, pace-friendly,
  spin-unfriendly. Favours teams with strong pace attacks and explosive
  batting.
