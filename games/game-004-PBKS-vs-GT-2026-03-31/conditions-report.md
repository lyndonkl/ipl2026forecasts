## Pitch Profile

- **Pitch type:** Balanced — good pace and bounce, batting-friendly with steady carry
- **Composition:** Red soil with natural grass. Sand-based outfield (Bermuda grass, super-fast).
- **Expected behavior:**
  - **Early overs (1-6):** Good pace and carry. Seam movement possible for quality pacers. Ball comes on to the bat — rewards aggressive stroke-play.
  - **Middle overs (7-15):** Surface may slow slightly. Spin gets more grip as the pitch dries. Wrist-spinners (Chahal, Rashid) become especially dangerous.
  - **Death overs (16-20):** High-variance phase. Dew (if evening) makes bowling harder. Short square boundaries (64-69m) punish any errors. Death bowling execution is the decisive skill at this venue.
- **Historical average first innings score:** ~170 (169.37 across 8 IPL matches)
- **Par score:** 170-180. Scores 180+ have strong success rate. 200+ rarely chased.
- **Highest total:** 228/5 (MI vs GT, IPL 2025 Eliminator)
- **Lowest total:** 95 all out (KKR vs PBKS, April 2025 — lowest defended in IPL history)
- **Score range:** Extremely wide (95-228). This venue can produce anything.

### Key bowling patterns at this venue:
- **Pace dominates:** 62% of overs bowled by pace, 142 wickets vs 51 for spin (3:1 ratio)
- **Short-pitch fast bowling** particularly effective (Arshdeep 4/29, Bumrah 3/21)
- **Wrist spin CAN succeed:** Chahal 4/28 (vs KKR, 2025), Sai Kishore 4/29 (vs PBKS, 2024)
- **Rashid Khan factor:** Rashid has not played at this venue in IPL before (GT played PBKS at Ahmedabad in 2025). His effectiveness here is an unknown.

## Toss and Conditions

- **Bat first vs chase:** 5-4 in favour of batting first (55%) — unusual for IPL. This is one of few venues where batting first is advantaged.
- **Toss advantage:** Moderate. The bat-first edge may make toss winner choose to bat — counter to most IPL venues.
- **Weather (March 31):** 22-27°C, cloudy. Rain forecast ahead of match start but no rain expected during match hours. Pre-match rain could affect pitch moisture.
- **Dew factor:** Onset after ~9:00 PM IST. Moderate severity. Ball skids on, spinners lose grip. For an evening game (7:30 PM start), dew arrives in second innings (overs 15-20 of chase). However, pre-match rain may reduce dew accumulation.
- **Wind:** Light (8-12 km/h), minimal impact.
- **Humidity:** Low to moderate (16-29%). Semi-dry climate.

### Toss prediction:
This is a genuinely difficult toss call. Historical bat-first advantage (55%) pulls toward batting first, but IPL convention and mild dew suggest bowling first. **Prediction: Toss winner likely bats first** given the venue's unusual bat-first record. But either decision is defensible.
