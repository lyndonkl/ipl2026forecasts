**Actual outcome:** SRH won

**Calculation:**
- Brier = (0.505 − 1)² = (−0.495)² = **0.2450**

- **0.2450 is equivalent to a coin flip.** Benchmark: 0.00 (perfect), 0.15 (good), 0.25 (coin flip), 1.00 (maximally wrong).
