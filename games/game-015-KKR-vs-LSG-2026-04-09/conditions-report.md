**Severity:** Heavy to very heavy. Humidity rising from 75–80% (start) to 85–90% (end of match), with temperature drop from ~28°C to ~24–26°C = classic dew acceleration profile.

**Impact on second innings bowling:**
- Spinners lose grip significantly. Varun, Ashton Agar (LSG), and other turn bowlers will be less effective than typical even on a flat surface.
- Pace bowlers lose reverse swing and conventional seam movement. Death bowlers will rely on yorkers and cutters rather than movement-based deliveries.
- Fielders: wet ball increases drop/misfiled risk. Catching becomes harder.
- **Net effect:** Second-innings batting advantage substantial. Chasing is favoured.
