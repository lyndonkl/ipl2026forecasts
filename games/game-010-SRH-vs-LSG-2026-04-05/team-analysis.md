- **Vulnerability:** If Ishan gets out, Mendis/Livingstone must anchor. Mendis (SL) may struggle against left-arm spin (LSG's Kulkarni or Hasaranga), though Hasaranga is more of an all-rounder here.

**Death Overs (Overs 16–20):**

**Overall Batting Assessment:** SRH's aggressive blueprint (Head, Abhishek, Klaasen) is ideally suited to this flat, batting-friendly pitch. They have the firepower to post 190–200+ if top 4 clicks. Cummins' absence doesn't materially affect batting depth.

### 4. Phase-by-Phase Bowling Assessment — SRH

**Powerplay Bowling (Overs 1–6):**
- **Primary bowlers:** Jaydev Unadkat (LHB seam) + Shivam Mavi or Eshan Malinga (RHB pace).
- **Expected economy:** 7.0–8.0 RPO. Target 42–48 runs with 1–2 wicket hauls.
- **Conditions fit:** Red clay surface with early seam and bounce assists pace. Unadkat's left-arm angle across right-hand Pant is a strong matchup.
- **Vulnerability:** LSG's Rishabh Pant is a destructive left-hander who doesn't respect seam; he attacks from ball one (SR 150+ in powerplay, career). Marsh (RHB) is a tough customer as well.
- **Expected outcome:** SRH likely to leak runs vs LSG's aggressive opening pair (Pant + Marsh) unless bowling is extremely tight.

**Middle Overs Bowling (Overs 7–15):**
- **Primary bowlers:** Harsh Dubey (left-arm wrist-spin, containment) + Nitish Kumar Reddy (seam all-rounder, 2–3 overs).
- **Expected economy:** 8.0–9.0 RPO. Target 72–81 runs with 2–3 wicket hauls.
- **Conditions fit:** Pitch flattens; spinners are not dominant wicket-takers here. Dubey's role is containment and dot-ball generation.
- **Vulnerability:** **SRH's spin bowling is a significant weakness.** Harsh Dubey is not a frontline T20 spinner like Kuldeep or Varun Chakravarthy. LSG's middle order (Pooran, Markram, Badoni) will target him. No elite off-spinner available to control right-handers.
- **Expected outcome:** SRH will likely concede 80–95 runs in this phase, with Dubey and NKR unable to create major breakthrough pressure.

**Death Overs Bowling (Overs 16–20):**
- **Primary bowlers:** Harshal Patel (death specialist) + Jaydev Unadkat or Shivam Mavi (pace).
- **Expected economy:** 9.0–11.0 RPO. Target 45–55 runs with 2 wickets.
- **Conditions fit:** Consistent bounce persists; Harshal's slower-ball yorkers are highly effective on this surface.
- **Vulnerability:** If Harshal is off-form or leaks in one over, SRH lack a second elite death bowler to rely on. Eshan Malinga is inexperienced.
- **Expected outcome:** With Harshal executing well, SRH can restrict death phase to 45–50 runs. Without him, 55–65 is realistic.

**Overall Bowling Assessment:** SRH's bowling is their weak link. **Without Pat Cummins, they lack a frontline strike bowler and captain's leadership.** Harshal Patel is world-class at death, but spin bowling (Dubey) is a containment-only option. Against LSG's firepower (Pant, Pooran, Shami, Nortje), SRH will need disciplined bowling execution.

**Key Bowling Balance:** 4 pace bowlers (Unadkat, Mavi, Malinga, Harshal) + 1 spinner (Dubey). Pace-heavy, but spinner lacks elite quality.
