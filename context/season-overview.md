## Tournament Structure

- **Duration:** March 28 – May 31, 2026
- **Total league matches:** 74
- **Teams:** 10
- **Opening match:** RCB vs SRH at M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bengaluru
  (March 28)
- **Format:** Two groups. Teams play once against teams in their own
  group and twice against teams from the opposite group.
- **Playoffs:** Qualifier 1 (1st vs 2nd), Eliminator (3rd vs 4th),
  Qualifier 2 (Eliminator winner vs Q1 loser), Final.
- **Venues:** 13 stadiums across India

### Phase breakdown

- Phase 1 (March 28 – April 12): 20 matches across 10 venues
- Phase 2 (April 13 – May 24): 50 matches across 12 venues
- Playoffs: TBA

### Rule Changes for IPL 2026

- **Overseas player cap:** Max 4 overseas in Playing XI (7 Indian players
  required) — unchanged from previous years.
- **Impact Player rule:** Overseas player can only be used as Impact
  Player if the starting XI has fewer than 4 overseas players.
- **Overseas salary cap:** Auction bids above INR 18 cr have the excess
  deposited into the BCCI Player Welfare Fund.
- **Withdrawal penalties:** Players withdrawing post-auction without
  verified medical reason face a two-season ban.
