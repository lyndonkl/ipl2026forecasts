## Venue Profile
- **Capacity:** ~55,000
- **Home team:** Sunrisers Hyderabad
- **Pitch type:** Flat batting surface — true bounce, consistent carry, minimal turn
- **Pitch composition:** Black soil with red soil overlay — firm, even bounce
- **Boundaries:** Standard IPL dimensions; large outfield requires efficient running between wickets

## Key Characteristics
- Consistently described as one of the most batter-friendly surfaces in India
- True bounce and consistent carry suit pace bowling more than spin
- Minimal turn — spinners must rely on pace variation and guile rather than the surface
- Pace-friendly early (new ball), flattens further as match progresses
- Large outfield rewards running and punishes mistimed lofted shots
- High-scoring venue — favours strong batting lineups and disciplined death bowling

## Toss and Conditions
- **Chase win rate:** ~60% (chasing favoured, dew-assisted)
- **Toss winner preference:** Slight lean toward bowling first; 47%/53% bat/bowl split historically
- **Toss advantage level:** Low to moderate — chase-favoured due to dew
- **Dew factor (mid-April):** Significant. Onset ~20:30–21:00 IST (8:30–9:00 PM). Dew becomes pronounced from the 12th–14th over of the second innings. Reduces grip for bowlers, assists stroke-making for batters. Chasing teams historically benefit.
- Wind: Light (13–15 km/h from south/southeast in evening). Minimal impact on play.

## Bowler/Batter Profiles That Succeed
- **Batters:** Aggressive stroke-makers thrive on the true bounce. Pace onto the bat suits wristwork and pull shots. Surface rewards timing over power.
- **Pace bowlers:** Back-of-length discipline rewarded. Early swing possible in first 2-3 overs (evening conditions, slight cloud cover). Elite death bowling economy is the critical differentiator at this venue — flat surface + dew = death bowling under pressure.
- **Spinners:** Minimal turn from the surface. Wrist spinners with pace variation can contain but won't dominate. Leg-spin that flights the ball above the eyeline can extract uncertainty; finger spin is largely nullified.
- **Key note:** Teams with elite death bowling units (Archer/Burger-type pairings) have a structural advantage at this venue due to dew making second-innings bowling harder.

- Humidity: 30–45% (moderate for Hyderabad)
- Cloud cover: Partly cloudy, minimal rain risk
- Visibility: Clear evening conditions, no fog/mist concerns
