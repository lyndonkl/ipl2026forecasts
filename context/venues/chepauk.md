## Venue Profile
- **Capacity:** ~50,000
- **Home team:** Chennai Super Kings
- **Pitch type:** Fresh turning track with light grass (IPL 2026 renovation surface); varies significantly match-to-match
- **Pitch composition:** Red soil with strong spin characteristics; recently renovated for T20 World Cup 2026
- **Boundaries:** Square boundaries relatively long (~70m); straight boundaries longer, favours placement over power

## Key Characteristics

- **Spin-dominant venue:** Historically one of the strongest spin-friendly surfaces in IPL. CSK have built historically spin-heavy squads to exploit these conditions.
- **Pitch variability:** Fresh pitches can be seaming; used pitches become dry turning tracks or slow and low. One of the hardest venues to predict due to match-to-match variability.
- **Surface renovation impact:** T20 World Cup 2026 renovation may have changed pitch behaviour compared to pre-renovation averages. WC matches averaged 197 runs (higher than historical IPL 155-170 pattern).
- **Coastal humidity:** Red soil pitch combined with coastal proximity affects dew patterns and surface characteristics.
- **Square boundary quirk:** Relatively long square boundaries reward stroke-placement and tactical batting over pure power-hitting.

## Toss and Conditions

- **Toss advantage:** Moderate, but advantage flips depending on pitch age
  - Fresh pitch: Bowling first is preferred (slight seam movement early)
  - Used pitch: Batting first is preferred (surface deteriorates, becomes dry and turning)
- **Dew onset:** Later than Mumbai/Bangalore — 8:30-9:00 PM
- **Dew intensity:** Moderate (less reliable predictor than coastal venues like Wankhede or Eden Gardens)
- **Impact on toss:** Dew arrives late enough that it rarely dominates match outcomes at Chepauk unlike other IPL venues

## Bowler/Batter Profiles That Succeed

**Bowlers who excel:**
- Spin bowlers (off-spin and leg-spin): Chepauk's red soil offers turn, especially on used pitches
- Left-arm pace: Gets assistance from angle across right-handers
- Mystery spinners and variations: CSK history shows effectiveness of Narine-type bowlers here
- Notable: Ravichandran Ashwin historically had strong records on Chepauk's turning surfaces

**Batters who excel:**
- Placement-based batters: Square boundaries reward timing and positioning over power
- Stroke-makers with spin competency: Surface demands technical batting as pitch wears
- Running between wickets: Large outfield and specific boundary placements reward quick singles
