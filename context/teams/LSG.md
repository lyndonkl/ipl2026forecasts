## Team Style

### Batting approach
Pant opens aggressively — left-handed, unorthodox, capable of
destroying any bowling attack in the powerplay. Pooran provides
explosive middle-order power. Marsh adds stability and power at 4–5.
Badoni is a talented young Indian batter developing rapidly. The
lineup has match-winners but can be inconsistent.

### Bowling approach
The pace battery is potentially the most fearsome in IPL 2026.
Shami provides swing and seam at 140 kph. Nortje bowls at 150+ kph.
Mayank Yadav is India's express pace sensation (155+ kph). Avesh Khan
adds depth. Hasaranga provides leg-spin with a strong googly. This
is a genuinely scary bowling attack — if Shami, Nortje, and Mayank
are all fit and firing simultaneously.

  the fastest bowling combination in IPL history. All three can bowl
  145–155+ kph consistently. Shami elite form sustained (G010 2/9, G015 eco 5.0, G019 continued form).
- **Abdul Samad Young Talent Ceiling (G019 Update):** Emerging finisher threat. G015 42 off 28 (SR 150, form surge) + G019 42 off 21 (SR 200) establish young batter trajectory. Ceiling rising, now 150–170 SR expected range (vs prior 125–135). High-impact finisher edge.
- **Wanindu Hasaranga Dual-Value Fit (G019 Update):** Post-hamstring fitness cleared. Batting SR 211 G015, SR 200 G019 (not dew-dependent, repeatable). Leg-spin edge vs GT RHB batters (Gill, Buttler, Sudharsan) EM/LM effectiveness. Bowling + batting pairing threat confirmed.

  powerplay is devastating. Also an excellent keeper. Form dip (G019 18 off 24, SR 75) shows afternoon heat + Rashid aggression can suppress performance but not eliminated.

  flexibility.

pace is less effective. Hasaranga's spin is useful if the pitch turns.
LSG need to assess the Lucknow surface match-by-match.
