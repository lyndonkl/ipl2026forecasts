## Team Style

### Batting approach
Aggressive, top-heavy. Kohli and Phil Salt form one of the most explosive
opening pairs in the IPL. Salt is a powerplay destructor (SR 160+).
Kohli anchors with ability to accelerate. Tim David is a death-over
specialist. Bethell adds left-handed variety.

### Bowling approach
Hazlewood (when fit) + Bhuvneshwar provide experienced new-ball swing/seam.
Shepherd offers pace at the death. Krunal is the primary spin option.
Lacks a frontline wrist-spinner — issue on turning tracks.

---

## Strengths

- **Batting depth:** Salt, Kohli, Patidar, Bethell, Tim David — genuine
  match-winners batting through 5.
- **Champion mentality:** IPL 2025 winners. Proven under pressure.
- **Chinnaswamy:** Flat, high-scoring home ground matches batting-heavy
  approach.

## Vulnerabilities

- **Bowling without Hazlewood:** Pace attack thin. Bhuvneshwar (36+)
  is aging. Yash Dayal out for season.
- **No wrist-spinner:** Krunal as primary spinner is a weakness on
  turning tracks.
- **Raipur unknown:** 2 matches at an unfamiliar secondary venue.

---

## Home Venue Fit

Chinnaswamy's flat, short-boundary surface is ideal for RCB's batting.
Six-hitting is easier here. But also brutal for bowlers — RCB's attack
will be tested in defence.
