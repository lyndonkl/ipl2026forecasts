## Team Style

### Batting approach
CSK traditionally bat conservatively in the powerplay and accelerate
from overs 10 onward. Ruturaj is a classical timer. Samson adds
explosive intent. Shivam Dube is the left-handed power option. Dhoni
remains the ultimate finisher in short cameos.

### Bowling approach
Loss of Jadeja and Curran significantly weakens all-round depth.
Akeal Hosein provides left-arm spin; Rahul Chahar is the wrist-spin
option. Pace relies on Matt Henry and Khaleel Ahmed. Spin remains
the strategic focus at Chepauk.

---

## Strengths

- **Chepauk mastery:** CSK's home record is elite. Fleming and Dhoni
  understand how to exploit Chepauk's conditions.
- **Tactical intelligence:** Dhoni's influence on field placements
  and bowling changes remains invaluable.
- **Samson acquisition:** Explosive batting addition who can win games
  single-handedly.

## Vulnerabilities

- **Pace bowling depth:** Nathan Ellis injured, post-trade losses.
  Matt Henry and Khaleel carry heavy responsibility.
- **Squad transition:** Losing Jadeja and Curran changes the team's
  DNA. New squad balance is unproven.
- **Dhoni's age:** At 44, extended batting under pressure is uncertain.

---

## Home Venue Fit

Chepauk's spin-friendly conditions suit CSK's historical approach but
this squad has fewer spin weapons than previous iterations. The loss
of Jadeja is significant. Matt Henry's seam bowling could be effective
on Chepauk's fresh-pitch green tops.

## Recent Seasons

- **IPL 2024:** Defended 2023 title poorly. Inconsistent.
