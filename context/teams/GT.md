## Team Style

### Batting approach
Strong top 3 — Gill, Buttler, Sudharsan. Gill is elegant and
increasingly aggressive in T20s. Buttler is a proven IPL match-winner
(4 centuries in IPL 2022). Sudharsan was the leading run-scorer in
IPL 2025 (759 runs). The top order is world-class. Middle order relies
on Rashid for quick cameos and Tewatia for death-over finishing.

### Bowling approach
Rashid Khan is the crown jewel — best T20 leg-spinner in the world.
Economy routinely under 7.0 and takes crucial wickets. Siraj provides
pace in the powerplay. Prasidh Krishna adds bounce and pace. Washington
Sundar bowls economical off-spin. Holder provides death-over pace if
selected. Bowling depth is solid but pace beyond Siraj and Prasidh is
a concern.

---

## Strengths

- **Rashid Khan:** Best T20 leg-spinner globally. Effective on every
  surface. Consistently economical and takes wickets in the middle overs.
- **Top-3 quality:** Gill, Buttler, Sudharsan is one of the strongest
  top orders in the tournament.
- **Captaincy:** Gill has grown into a capable leader. Nehra's tactical
  approach complements Gill's instincts.

## Vulnerabilities

- **Middle-order depth after top 3:** If Gill, Buttler, and Sudharsan
  all fail, the middle order (Phillips/Shahrukh, Rashid, Tewatia) is
  untested as a unit.
- **Pace bowling depth:** Beyond Siraj and Prasidh, options are limited.
  Ishant Sharma (37+) is aging. Prithvi Raj is unproven.
- **Buttler's IPL consistency:** Buttler has boom-or-bust IPL seasons —
  either dominates or underperforms.

---

## Home Venue Fit

Narendra Modi Stadium's enormous outfield suits Rashid's bowling (harder
to hit boundaries) and GT's emphasis on fielding and running between
wickets. Multiple pitch blocks mean GT must be versatile — Rashid is
effective on any surface, but the pace bowlers need pitches with pace
to be dangerous.

## Recent Seasons

- **IPL 2025:** Strong season. Sudharsan won Orange Cap (759 runs).
  Prasidh Krishna won Purple Cap (25 wickets). Good campaign but fell
  short of the final.
- **IPL 2024:** Disappointing. Failed to qualify for playoffs after
  winning the title in 2022.
